Campaign
Overview

This campaign tested multiple static ad creatives for an orthopedic shoe product, focusing on comfort, support, and everyday walking pain points.

The goal was to improve click-through rate and conversion intent by testing cleaner visuals, stronger hooks, and more direct response messaging.

KEY FOCUS AREAS

Image Ads

Static creatives designed to communicate comfort and support quickly.

CTR Testing

Testing hooks, visuals, and angles to improve click-through rate.

Direct Response

Messaging built around pain relief, walking comfort, and product benefits.
